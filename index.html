My Story 2020

दिल ना उमीद तो नहीं नाकाम ही तो है, लंबी है ग़म की शाम मगर शाम ही तो है|

Khanai likhne pata nai kyu itna muskil se lagte hai per theek hai karte hai thoda un waqto ke yaad. 2019 ka saal ke mera ek kharab start ke badd ek peek pe gaye phir nicche aa gaya. Kaffi dosti tutt rakhe the pichle dinno me naye saal me umeed to bhot thi zindagi se. Yesha tha ki sayad ye saal bhot kuch solve kar dega mere life me atleast family problems to kaffi hadd tak resolve ho hi jayege. Saal ki surat me hi elections ke kamm the election accha gujara naye dost banne bhot kuch sikha tha phle mahine ek hassi khushi ke sath january gujar gaye. Dekhte dekhte mid sem aa gaya kuch puarne riste phirse se laga ki sahi ho jayenge per sayad kuch waisa hona nai tha. Mid sem gujre uske badd time the holi ka aur maine phle barr decide kiya the ghar nai janee ka decision kaffi sahi hi raha tha q ki padhai ho rahe the kuch apne acche dost bhi the break me sath and koi mil bhi gaye the batte karne ko apna sab kuch jisse suna sakte the, so sahi gujar rahe the holi gaye aur aa gaya COVID aur yeha se suru hua saal ka downfall kahir campus se ghar bhaga diya gaya aur phir lockdown ho gaye zindagi shanddar chal rahe the actually padhai ho rahi thi naye chize sikh rahe the aur ek khanai agge bhi badh rahi thi lockdown mai CEO bann gaya EC ka ek responsibility le liya tha abbr. Khair mujhe pata tha team banne mere liye unti muskil nai hogi aur hua waisa hi. Hnn mai batna bhul gaya mai nani ghar me rheta hu and mere family bhi nani ke sath hi thead last 1 saal se q ki nani ghar pe akle thi... so lockdown me ramyana ke gyan mila aur family ke sath gujarne ka time. yeshe jaldi se August aa gaye jaha campus bhen ne ghar pe rakhi bheje th jo mere liye na janne kitne bade batt the. se suru hue so called worst time of my life. Hamre family situation kuch theek nai thi na hi papa ka kuch kamm chal raha tha march se mummy ko kuch prlbs hai halat yeshe thi jaha harr time tension tha jaha samagh nai aa raha tha kya kare q ki agle 3 mahine me placement hai mere mummy hospitalized hone ke halat me thi pata nai tha kab hame chod dege paise nai the hamre pass aur koi support nai tha. at last mummy papa bhen nani ghar chod ke ek family friend ke yeha chale gaye harr waqt tension uske sath padhai yesha lag raha tha kya chal raha hai jeevan. actually mai words me bata nai sakta wo feeling uss lachar halat me mai mummy papa se durr ho gaya tha ya phir yesha kaho mai padhai ke chakkar me unhe sab chizon se ladne chod diya tha. ab mere mummy se bhi batt nai ho rahe thi na hi nani ghar me kisi se batt karne ka mann kar raha tha aur nai hi koi aur tha jisse mai apne khanai bata sakta tha. Kahir september gujara rone ke alwa koi aur option nai tha harr din chupchap apne rona sayad addat se ho gaye the. Khair kuch dinno me mummy wapas dadi ghar chale gaye the jaha mai last 5 saal se ek ratt nai raha hu. dekhte dekhte december ka time aa gaya tha palcement time. ab yesha lag raha tha hnn sayad saal sahi kahtam hoga placement lag jayege per jo hua sayad zindagi bhar nai bhul paunga test acche ja rahe the yesha lag raha tha day 3 tak to ho hi jayega placement bina koi dikkat ke. Day 1 me no interview till day 3 koi interview nai hue the uss time ke liye ek sabad tha intzaar sayad inti himmat kabhi aur rakhne ke jaurat nai pade the hnn the kuch log jo kahde the thanks to them jinke wajah se aaj zinda hu q ki mere me itne himmat nai thi ki uss sab se akle lad pata kahir palcement nai lage ab zindagi pe pata nai kya karunga jo chiz soch ke baitha tha sab tutta hua nazar aya sare plans khatam hote hue nazar aye pata nai kya karunga ye sabse bada question tha uss time kahir end sem khatam hua aur phir jo kuch log ka sath tha wo bhi chod diye. saal ka ant atte atte yesha lagne laga tha why i am even alive?? per theek hai. Ek chiz sikha tha iss saal se kisi pe blindly trust matt karo harr koi ek maksad ke liye aapke sath khada hota hai. And family bass mummy papa hote hai bakki to sab u hi hai. Paise insann ko bhot badal dete hai pata nai kya hoga mera iss saal dekhte hai kaha le jayege 2021 mujhe.
